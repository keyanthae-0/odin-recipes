Tres Leches Cake


Description

Tres leches cake is a soft, cloud-like dessert that feels almost weightless when you take a bite. Baked as a simple sponge cake,
it becomes something magical once it’s soaked in a mixture of three milks—evaporated milk, sweetened condensed milk, and heavy cream.
The result is a dessert that stays incredibly moist without ever feeling soggy. Every forkful delivers a cool, creamy sweetness that spreads across your palate,
making this cake refreshing, satisfying, and surprisingly light despite its richness.


Ingredients

Cake Ingredients
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
Milk Texture [Tres Leches]
  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or whole milk
Topping
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
