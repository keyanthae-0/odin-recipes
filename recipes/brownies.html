Brownies


Description

Brownies are one of those timeless desserts that feel like comfort in every bite. They’re dense, chocolaty squares with a soft center that melts just slightly under the warmth of your fingers.
The aroma alone—deep cocoa mixed with a hint of butter—can turn an ordinary day into something a little sweeter. Each bite gives that perfect blend of chewiness around the edges and velvety softness in the middle,
with a rich flavor that satisfies any chocolate craving.


Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven:
    • to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8×8 baking dish or line it with parchment paper.
  2. Mix Wet Ingredients:
    • In a bowl, stir together melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Add Dry Ingredients:
    • Gently fold in the cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder. Mix just until combined.
  4. Customize:
    • Add chocolate chips or nuts if you want extra texture.
  5. Bake:
    • Pour the batter into the baking dish and spread evenly. Bake for 20–25 minutes, or until the center is set but still soft.
  6. Cool and Slice:
    • Let the brownies cool before cutting into squares for clean edges.
